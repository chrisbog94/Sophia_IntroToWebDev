Community Involvement

At Bloom Valley Nursery, we believe in giving back to the community that has supported us for years.

  • School Garden Program: We donate plants and supplies to local elementary schools to support outdoor learning gardens.
  • Neighborhood Beautification: We partner with the city to provide plants for public parks and streetscapes.
  • Composting Workshops: Free monthly workshops on composting and sustainable gardening practices.
  • Plant Swap Events: Quarterly community plant swaps where gardeners can trade cuttings and seeds.
